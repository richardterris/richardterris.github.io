Blogging about blogging

June 9 2018

I've always had trouble with the idea of blogging. There. I've said it.


It's not that I have a problem with other people blogging, or indeed about blogging itself. Really the issue I've had is the idea of ME blogging!
I suppose I've always wondered what I have to contribute, what do I have to say that's so important that anyone else should read?


I was wrong - I missed the point

What I started to realise recently, particularly from watching some other better known software engineers in the community is that there is room for EVERYONE.
Even if ten other people have written blogs about the same topic, it doesn't matter because it's your version of it.


People still write love songs even
though Paul McCartney and Stevie
Wonder have written so many!

The point I've realised is that with blogging, making music, painting, making movies, anything creative, is that first and foremost you do it for YOURSELF. If other people find what you say useful or interesting then that's a bonus but that shouldn't be the reason for doing it.


It's thanks to this realisation that I've decided to start blogging. Most of my blogs will be technical as I expand my skills and experience with various technologies, but I might also write posts on anything; updates on the album I've recorded, my photography hobby as it progresses, thoughts and ramblings about life. Whatever.


If you like it for any reason, then that's great. If you don't well, don't read it.
