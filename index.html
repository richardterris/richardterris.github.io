Hello, I am Richard

Software Developer from Glasgow, UK

I'm a contract software developer, working on the .NET framework. I have experience in the full software development lifecycle, building content managed websites, custom software applications, and 3rd party integrations.

I love writing clean, extensible, and maintainable code, and I especially love writing simple code to solve complex problems for the end user. At the moment I'm really enjoying the experience I'm getting with Azure, particularly Functions, Microservices with Service Fabric, and many other great features that Azure has, like table storage, search, and plugins like Redis Cache; it's exciting stuff!

When I'm not working I am usually either playing/recording music, walking my dog, hill walking, or taking photos. If I can combine 2 or more of these things at once then I'm really happy.
